What is the purpose of the website?
The site provides a comprehensive overview of all characters and maps in the game "Brawl Stars."
What does the color around the Brawler's image mean?
The color around the image indicates the rarity of the Brawler.
Will new Brawlers/Maps be added directly in the future?
Yes, once the data for the Brawler/Map is available in the API, it will be displayed on this website immediately.
Where can I learn more about the API?
You can find a detailed introduction under "BrawlAPI."
